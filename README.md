## Editing

Everything lives in `index.html` — HTML, CSS, and a couple of inline SVGs. Search for these placeholder sections to customise:

- **Roster** — replace the "Architect / Scribe / Champion" cards with your actual team members
- **Honours** — update with your real events, dates, and results
- **Bot specs** — swap in your actual robot's drivetrain, intake, lift design
- **Contact** — change `hello@example.com` and add your social links
- **Motto & taglines** — make them yours

## Stack

Pure HTML + CSS. No frameworks. Fonts from Google Fonts (Cinzel, Fraunces, JetBrains Mono).
